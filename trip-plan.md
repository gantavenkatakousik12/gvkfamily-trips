## 📅 DAY-BY-DAY ITINERARY (7 Days)

### DAY 1 (Night Departure from Bhimavaram/Vijayawada)
- **Evening:** Travel Bhimavaram → Vijayawada Junction
- **Night (03:40 AM Day 2):** Board AP Express (20805) or Tamil Nadu SF (12621) from BZA
- **Travel Duration:** ~26 hours to Delhi

---

### DAY 2 (On Train)
- Full day on train
- Rest, enjoy scenic views through Central India
- **Option:** Board Duronto (12269) if departing Day 1 at 12:20 PM from BZA (arrives Delhi next morning)

---

### DAY 3 — DELHI ARRIVAL + DELHI SIGHTSEEING (Optional)
- **Morning:** Arrive New Delhi (NDLS / NZM)
- **Morning/Afternoon:** Quick Delhi sightseeing if time allows:
  - India Gate (30 min)
  - Qutub Minar or Red Fort (1–2 hrs)
  - Chandni Chowk for food (1 hr)
- **Evening/Night:** Board Vande Bharat (22436, 06:00 NDLS) **next morning** OR overnight Shiv Ganga (12560, 20:05) to Varanasi

> **Best Pick:** Board Shiv Ganga at 20:05 → arrive Varanasi 06:10 Day 4 (saves hotel night in Delhi)

---

### DAY 4 — ARRIVE VARANASI + EVENING GANGA AARTI
- **Morning (06:10 AM):** Arrive Varanasi Banaras (BNRS) station
- **Morning:** Check-in hotel near Dashashwamedh Ghat area
- **Morning 06:30–08:00:** Sunrise boat ride on Ganges ⭐ (₹200–₹400/person shared, ₹800–₀1,500 private for group)
- **08:00–10:00:** Kashi Vishwanath Temple darshan (arrive early to beat crowds)
- **10:00–13:00:** Manikarnika Ghat, Assi Ghat walk
- **Afternoon:** Rest at hotel
- **17:00–18:00:** Get ready for Ganga Aarti
- **18:00–19:30:** Dashashwamedh Ghat — **Grand Ganga Aarti** ⭐⭐ (most spectacular)
- **Night:** Stay in Varanasi (near ghats)

---

### DAY 5 — FULL VARANASI + DEPART TO AYODHYA NIGHT
- **05:30–07:00:** Early morning Ghat walk (Assi → Dashashwamedh → Manikarnika)
- **07:00–08:00:** Morning Aarti at Assi Ghat (quieter, spiritual)
- **08:00–10:00:** Sarnath Buddhist site (10 km from Varanasi) — Dhamek Stupa, Deer Park
- **10:30–12:00:** Kashi Vishwanath Corridor visit (if not done Day 4), Annapurna Temple
- **12:00–13:00:** Lunch (famous Kashi kachori/chaat at Deena Chat Bhandar)
- **13:00–15:00:** BHU New Vishwanath Temple, Durga Kund Mandir, Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple
- **15:00–17:00:** Ramnagar Fort (cross Ganges by boat — beautiful fort)
- **18:00–20:00:** Evening Ganga Aarti (Day 2, now you know the flow — enjoy front seats)
- **21:00 or 22:00:** Depart Varanasi → Ayodhya (overnight bus or cab, ~5 hrs)

> **Cab option:** Book private cab Varanasi → Ayodhya: ₹2,500–₀4,000 total for group (arrives by 03:00–04:00 AM)
> **Bus option:** UPSRTC last bus ~22:00, ₹338–₀485/person

---

### DAY 6 — AYODHYA (Full Day Pilgrimage)
- **05:00 AM:** Reach Ayodhya, freshen up at hotel/dharamshala
- **05:30 AM:** Hanuman Garhi Temple ⭐ (visit before Ram Mandir — tradition)
  - 76 steps climb, view of entire Ayodhya from top
- **07:00–09:00:** **Ram Janmabhoomi / Ram Mandir** ⭐⭐⭐
  - **Book Sugam Darshan online FREE** at srjbtkshetra.in (saves long queues)
  - Nagara architecture, pink Rajasthan sandstone, Ram Lalla idol
- **09:30–10:30:** Kanak Bhawan — golden temple (gifted to Sita by Queen Kaikeyi)
- **10:30–11:30:** Dashrath Mahal (50m from Hanuman Garhi)
- **11:30–12:30:** Nageshwarnath Temple (oldest temple, built by Lord Ram's son Kush)
- **12:30–14:00:** Sita Ki Rasoi (mythological kitchen of Sita, near Ram Janmabhoomi)
- **14:00–15:00:** Lunch (free Prasad available at several temples 11:30–15:00)
- **15:00–16:30:** Ram Ki Paidi Ghats / Saryu River front walk
- **17:00–18:30:** Saryu River Ghat — **Evening Aarti** (similar to Varanasi Ganga Aarti)
- **19:00–20:00:** Guptar Ghat (where Lord Rama took Jal Samadhi)
- **Night:** Stay Ayodhya OR depart for Delhi/return

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT REMINDERS

1. **Ram Mandir:** Book Sugam Darshan e-pass FREE at srjbtkshetra.in — long queues without it
2. **Train booking:** Book ASAP on IRCTC — popular routes fill fast, especially 3AC
3. **Varanasi:** Carry original ID for some temple entry (Kashi Vishwanath)
4. **Dress code:** Cover shoulders and legs at all temples — carry a scarf/dupatta
5. **Manikarnika Ghat:** Respect the space, no photography of cremations
6. **Mobile restriction:** No phones inside Ram Mandir sanctum — keep in locker outside
7. **Beware touts:** Especially near Varanasi ghats — negotiate auto/boat fares upfront
8. **Best season:** Oct–March (cool weather) — ✅ current time is March, perfect weather!
